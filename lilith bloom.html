About Us

Lilith Bloom is a training program or workshop designed to teach people how to create and arrange various types of flowers into beautiful bouquets, arrangements, or flower decorations. In this class, participants typically learn about various types of flowers, different arranging techniques, color selection, and the aesthetics of flower design. The goal is to develop floral arranging skills so that participants can create captivating and artistic flower creations for various events or as a personal hobby. Flower arranging classes are often guided by experienced instructors in the art of floristry.
